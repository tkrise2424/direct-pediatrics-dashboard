Baseline Intelligence Summary — Dual-Channel

Nearly Newborn · MMai + Prosp · October 2025 – April 20, 2026

Nearly Newborn is running a true dual-channel outreach program — MMai driving Email and SMS volume at scale, and Prosp powering LinkedIn connection-based outreach. The most important signal from this baseline period is that LinkedIn is the highest-performing engagement channel: a 24.6% acceptance rate and 27.3% reply rate (of accepted) are strong indicators that the LinkedIn audience is well-matched and the messaging is resonating at the connection stage. In contrast, MMai's Email and SMS channels have confirmed deliverability (1.69% open rate across 3,781 executions) but have not yet produced clicks or replies — pointing to CTA and message body optimization as the primary lever for next period. Combined, the program has touched 3,923+ prospects across three channels, generated 9 LinkedIn replies as the first real conversation-level signals, and built an infrastructure of 2,310 intent-qualified contacts, 7 sequences, and a live AI agent across 4 channels. The optimization priority is clear: replicate LinkedIn's reply-driving approach in email messaging, activate the 333 pending approvals, and convert the 9 LinkedIn replies into booked consultations.

🏆 Top Performing Channel
LinkedIn (Prosp) is the clear leader this period — 24.6% acceptance rate and 27.3% reply rate of accepted contacts. These are strong cold-outreach benchmarks and indicate the LinkedIn audience targeting and connection messaging are working.
📬 Email — Deliverability Confirmed, Engagement Pending
1.69% open rate across 3,781 executions confirms inbox placement is working. The gap is below the open — zero clicks, zero replies. The CTA needs to be rewritten to drive action, not just awareness.
🔗 Cross-Channel Opportunity
9 LinkedIn replies represent the warmest known prospects in the program. These contacts should be enrolled in an MMai follow-up sequence immediately — using email or SMS to reinforce the LinkedIn conversation and drive toward a booking.
⚡ Fastest Path to Meetings
Scaling LinkedIn volume (currently 142 requests) and adding a Calendly link directly into the LinkedIn follow-up message for the 9 active replies is the single fastest path to a booked consultation this period.

💡Performance Insights

Data-backed observations across both channels. Pattern-based — not generic.

1
LinkedIn is the standout channel — 27.3% reply rate is above industry average
A 27.3% reply rate among accepted LinkedIn connections is a strong signal that the audience targeting and connection message are working. Industry benchmarks for cold LinkedIn outreach typically sit at 10–15% reply rates. This means the "Direct Peds" campaign is reaching the right people with a message that resonates at the connection stage. The immediate priority is following up with all 9 replies and moving them toward a consultation booking.
2
Email deliverability is healthy — the CTA is the bottleneck, not the audience
64 unique opens confirm that emails are landing in inboxes. The 0% click and 0% reply rate points to a message body and CTA problem, not an audience problem. The current approach — a multi-paragraph intro with a bare link to the homepage — lacks urgency and a single clear action. A rewrite focused on one benefit and one link (e.g., free guide signup) is the highest-leverage email change available.
3
The 9 LinkedIn replies are the warmest leads in the entire program — they need immediate follow-up
These 9 contacts have accepted a connection, engaged in conversation, and replied — making them the highest-intent prospects identified to date. If they have not already received a follow-up with a booking link, that is the single most urgent action item from this report. Each day without follow-up reduces conversion probability.
4
SMS is generating noise — phone list quality is the root cause
The majority of AI agent SMS conversations are opt-outs, wrong numbers, and non-prospects. This is consistent with the DNC flags visible in the audience list data, where multiple phone numbers per contact are marked DNC. Scaling SMS without a phone data audit will increase opt-out rates and risk carrier flagging. SMS should be paused or reduced until list quality is confirmed.
5
Two large inactive audiences represent immediate scale potential
The Baby Prep Keywords list (1,117 contacts) and Gift Giver segments (825 contacts across 2 sequences) are built but dormant. Together they represent nearly 2,000 additional contacts ready to receive outreach — more than double the currently active audience — with no additional list-building required. Reactivating these with revised messaging could significantly increase total outreach volume and open rate data points in the next period.
