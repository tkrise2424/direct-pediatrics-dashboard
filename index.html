Performance Insights
Open rate is outperforming the healthcare industry benchmark by 2–4 points
A 12.21% open rate against a typical healthcare cold email benchmark of 8–10% indicates that subject lines are well-targeted and the sender domain (team@directpediatricsky.com and hannah@directpediatrics.com) is landing in primary inboxes — validated by the 100% warmup health score. This is a strong foundation to build on.
Reply rate is the critical gap — clicks are happening but not converting
110 unique clicks with only 1 reply reveals a disconnect between content engagement and response action. Prospects are reading and clicking — but the CTA or the ask is not compelling enough to drive a reply. This is the single highest-leverage optimization opportunity in the next cycle.
HR Benefits / B2B segment is the most active pipeline by volume
With 665 contacts loaded and 171 actively in-progress, the corporate employee benefits track (Steve) represents the deepest top-of-funnel pipeline. B2B corporate buyers also tend to have higher lifetime value for a concierge pediatrics practice — this segment warrants prioritization in messaging refinement.
Maternal/Postpartum and ADHD segments have elevated bounce rates
Both segments show 20 bounces each on relatively small sends (130 and 125 steps respectively), yielding bounce rates of ~15% within those sequences. This suggests the contact lists for these segments may include a higher proportion of outdated or unverified email addresses. List hygiene and re-sourcing should be evaluated before the next load.
Large queued contact pool represents significant near-term outreach capacity
722 contacts are loaded into active sequences but have not yet been contacted (Not Started status). As the sending cadence continues at current rate limits (30/day for team@, 15/day for hannah@), this pool will be activated over the next 4–6 weeks — creating a natural inflection point for engagement metrics in Report #2 and #3.

What We Are Working On to Improve Performance
🔴 Trigger
Reply rate is critically low at 0.02% despite a healthy 12.21% open rate and 110 clicks — indicating a CTA or ask problem, not a reach problem.
🔵 Action
Rewrite Step 2 and Step 3 CTAs across all active sequences to use a single, low-friction ask — e.g., "Would a 15-minute call make sense?" instead of longer value propositions.
🟢 Expected Outcome
Lift reply rate from 0.02% toward 0.5–1.5% within 2 reporting cycles. Even 5–10 replies from the current pipeline would represent a significant conversion improvement.
🔴 Trigger
Maternal/Postpartum and ADHD sequences show ~15% bounce rates within their early sends — well above the acceptable <2% threshold for list quality.
🔵 Action
Audit the contact source and email verification status for both segments. Re-run list hygiene via an email validation tool before loading additional contacts into these sequences.
🟢 Expected Outcome
Reduce sequence-level bounce rates to <3% and protect sender domain reputation. Improved list quality directly improves open and engagement rates for remaining contacts.
🔴 Trigger
722 contacts are queued but not yet contacted across active sequences — a significant untapped outreach pool that will activate over the next 4–6 weeks.
🔵 Action
Increase daily send limits incrementally (from 30 to 45 for team@, 15 to 25 for hannah@) as warmup health holds at 100% — accelerating the activation of queued contacts.
🟢 Expected Outcome
Reduce time-to-contact for queued prospects from 6 weeks to 3–4 weeks, generating more engagement data faster and compressing the feedback loop for optimization.
🔴 Trigger
LinkedIn (Prosp) campaigns are not yet active — leaving a high-value second touchpoint channel completely unused while email outreach runs.
🔵 Action
Launch Prosp LinkedIn campaigns targeting the HR Benefits and Referral Partner segments first — these B2B audiences are most reachable on LinkedIn and align with existing email sequences for multi-touch coverage.
🟢 Expected Outcome
Create a multi-touch funnel (email + LinkedIn) that increases total touchpoints per prospect by 2–3x, significantly improving reply and conversion rates for the B2B pipeline.
🔴 Trigger
AI agents (Lōvu + practice agents) are live but showing minimal conversation volume — the website traffic driving agent engagement is not yet measured or optimized.
🔵 Action
Add email capture to the Lōvu and practice agent conversations (lead field collection) and connect agent conversation outcomes to the MMai contact lists for sequence enrollment.
🟢 Expected Outcome
Convert website visitors who engage with AI agents into identified contacts that can be enrolled in nurture sequences — closing the loop between inbound interest and outbound follow-up.
